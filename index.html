About Me

Hello, my name is Tommy Newnum and I am a finance professional turned Full Stack Web Developer. With over eight years of experience in Financial Services, I have gained a deep understanding of financial markets and analysis, as well as a keen attention to detail.

Recently, I completed a Full Stack Web Development program from Michigan State University, where I learned various programming languages and tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MSQL.

During the program, I honed my skills in designing and developing web applications from scratch, writing clean and maintainable code, and using various frameworks to enhance user experience.

With my background in finance and newfound passion for web development, I am excited to apply my skills to create innovative and intuitive applications that solve real-world problems.
